What is Scanning and Indexing Services?

Document scanning and indexing is a process of tagging or connecting certain information with a file so that it can be used for various search and retrieval purposes in the future. In short, scanning and indexing is basically a path that leads to the document. Setting up of the indexes is considered to be one of the major factors for having a successful document management implementation.
