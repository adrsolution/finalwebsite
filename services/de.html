What is Data Entry?

A skill-based job wherein a person updates the information in the machine as per the requirements.

Why do we need it?

Data entry keeps all relevant information related to the business in one place and in correct order for reference. Data entry helps companies in reducing the cost of operations. A systematic process of database entry into computer systems also ensures confidentiality, which is a plus point in company matters.
