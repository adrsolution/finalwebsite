What is Web Searching?

Web research is the practice of using Web information, especially free information on the World Wide Web. Web research has had a profound impact on the way ideas are formed and knowledge is created. Common applications of Web research include personal research on a particular subject (something mentioned on the news, a health problem, etc.), students doing research for academic projects and papers, and journalists and other writers researching stories. Research is a broad term. Here, it is used to mean "looking something up (on the Web)". It includes any activity where a topic is identified, and an effort is made to actively gather information for the purpose of furthering understanding. It may include some post-collection analysis like a concern for quality or synthesis.
